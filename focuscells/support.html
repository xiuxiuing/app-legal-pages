Common Questions

Does FocusCells require an account?

No. The current version of FocusCells does not require an account or login.

Does FocusCells upload training records?

No. The current version processes training settings and session information on the user’s device and does not upload training records to our servers.

Does FocusCells include ads or third-party tracking?

No. The current version does not include third-party advertising or cross-app tracking technologies.

Is Beginner mode a children’s account or Kids Category feature?

No. Beginner mode is a simpler training experience with easier difficulty and softer feedback. It is not a child account, parental control system, school record system, or educational assessment.

Is FocusCells a medical or educational assessment tool?

No. FocusCells is a general focus, visual scanning, reaction speed, and Schulte table training app. It does not provide medical, psychological, clinical, or educational diagnosis or assessment.
