FocusCells Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 19, 2026

Product: FocusCells - Schulte Table, Focus & Reaction Trainer

Publisher: [LEGAL NAME OF DEVELOPER OR COMPANY]

Contact: [PRIVACY CONTACT EMAIL]

This Privacy Policy explains how FocusCells handles information when you use the mobile application. It is written for a global audience and should be reviewed by qualified privacy counsel before publication.

1. Short Summary

FocusCells is a focus, reaction, and Schulte table training app. The current version is designed to work without an account, advertising, social features, cloud sync, or third-party analytics SDKs.

Based on the current MVP implementation and PRD:

  • We do not ask users to create an account.
  • We do not ask for name, email address, age, school, contacts, photos, microphone, camera, precise location, or health records.
  • We do not sell personal information.
  • We do not use third-party advertising or third-party tracking.
  • Training settings and training results are intended to stay on the user's device.
  • Child Mode changes the training experience with larger numbers and softer feedback. It is not intended to collect children's personal information.

If future versions add accounts, cloud sync, analytics, advertising, payments, support forms, iCloud sync, or any third-party SDK that collects data, this policy and the App Store privacy disclosures must be updated before release.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information collected by the app

The current version of FocusCells does not require users to provide personal information.

The app may create training-related information on the user's device, such as:

  • Selected training mode, such as Adult or Child.
  • Selected grid difficulty, such as 3x3, 4x4, or 5x5.
  • Training timing, reaction pace, completion status, and mistake count.
  • Daily progress and recent training trend data.

This training information is used to operate the app experience and display progress to the user. In the current product design, it remains local to the device and is not uploaded to our servers.

2.2 Information users choose to send us

If a user contacts us by email or another support channel outside the app, we may receive the information they choose to provide, such as an email address, device details, screenshots, or a support message. We use that information only to respond to the request, troubleshoot issues, improve the product, or comply with legal obligations.

2.3 Apple-provided diagnostics and App Store information

Apple may provide developers with crash reports, diagnostics, sales, usage, or app analytics information through Apple developer tools or App Store Connect, depending on the user's device settings and Apple's policies. We use this information, if available, to understand app stability and improve FocusCells. Apple processes this information under its own terms and privacy practices.

3. Information We Do Not Collect

In the current version, FocusCells does not collect:

  • Contact information, such as name, email, phone number, or postal address, through the app.
  • Precise or approximate location.
  • Contacts, photos, videos, audio, camera, or microphone data.
  • Health, biometric, financial, or government identity information.
  • Advertising identifiers for tracking.
  • Browsing history, search history, or data from other apps.
  • Children's personal information.

4. How We Use Information

We use local training information to:

  • Run Schulte table training sessions.
  • Show the next target number, timer, progress, and completion summary.
  • Display daily progress and recent training trends.
  • Apply user-selected mode and difficulty settings.
  • Provide encouraging feedback and child-friendly training copy.

If a user contacts support, we use the provided contact information to:

  • Respond to the message.
  • Investigate bugs or support requests.
  • Maintain records needed for security, legal, or operational reasons.

6. Children's Privacy

FocusCells includes a Child Mode intended to provide a calmer, easier training experience with larger numbers and softer feedback. The current version does not ask for a child's name, age, school, contact information, location, photos, voice, or other personal information.

Parents and guardians should supervise children's use of the app. If you believe a child has sent personal information to us through a support channel, contact us at [PRIVACY CONTACT EMAIL] and we will take reasonable steps to delete it.

We do not sell personal information.

We do not share local training data with advertisers or data brokers.

  • With Apple as part of app distribution, App Store operation, and developer tools.
  • If required by law, legal process, or government request.
  • To protect the rights, safety, and security of users, the app, or others.
  • In connection with a business transfer, such as merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, subject to appropriate safeguards.

The current app is designed to store training information locally on the user's device. If users contact us or if Apple provides diagnostics or developer analytics, information may be processed in the country where we, Apple, or our service providers operate.

No method of storage or transmission is completely secure. Users should keep their device updated and protected with a passcode, Face ID, or Touch ID where available.
